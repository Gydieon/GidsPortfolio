Helpdesk Ticketing Systems

A deep dive series into industry standard helpdesk platforms showcasing hands on deployments, configurations, and comparisons of both open source and enterprise based tools used in real world support environments.

Why Helpdesk Systems?

Every IT support role relies on a ticketing system to track, prioritize, and resolve user issues efficiently at scale. Understanding how these platforms operate from an administrative and technical perspective, not just as an end user, is a core IT support competency. This series showcases full deployments and real system configurations rather than surface level usage.
